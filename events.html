Events

Monthly Events

Monthly Member / Business Meeting

When: First Wednesday of each month at 7 PM

Join us for updates, discussions, and community engagement.

Monthly Social – Kafichränzli

When: Third Saturday of each month at 11 AM

Informal coffee gathering at Ettore’s. Enjoy conversation, Swiss card games (Jass), and time with friends.

Monthly Walk / Hike & Picnic

When: Monthly starting in April at 9 AM

Bring a picnic and join us for easy to moderate walks followed by social time outdoors.
